Kunu Drink

Ingredients

  • 1.2g of Millet or guniea Corn
  • Tigernut (For Milky flavour)
  • 1 cup of Dates(Natural Sweetener)
  • Sweet Potatoes: Either Dried/ fresh (Reduces the thickness)
  • Ginger
  • Garlic Cloves/ Cinnamon

Preparation

  • Measure 10 (250g cups) of millet into a pot, with 1 cup of dates (Remove the seeds), and 1 cup of tigernut. Leave it to soak for about 8 Hours
  • Wrinse your millets, dates, and tigernut well, then pour them gradually into your blender. Add a bit of water to your blender to ease the blending process.
  • After blending, get a large pot and pour your millet mix to the pot.
  • Put your gas on medium heat and stirr to prevent lumps. NB: It takes time to thicken, just keep stirring.
  • After it has thickened to a desired consostency, turn of the heat and leave to coli.
  • Add your sugar, or honey if prefferd
  • Add iced block pellets to the kunu, or leave in the refrigerator
  • Enjoy your fresh kunu!!!

Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour 500g (4 cups)
  • Sugar 1 cup
  • Buter 50g
  • Yeast 1tbsp
  • Eggs 1 (No matter the amount or quantity of flour, your eggs should be 1.)
  • Milk 50g

    Ingredients for pizza toppings

  • Hot dogs
  • Bell Pepper
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Spring Onions

Preparation

  • Add all your dry ingredients to a bowl first to aviod lumps.
  • Then add your milk and butter to the flour and knead the dough on a flat surface.
  • Cut your dough into half and keep set one aside
  • Keep kneading the dough You would know when your dough is good when its less sticky
  • Sprinkle flour on the surface and keep kneading the dough.
  • Stop when its not sticky
  • Cover and set aside. Then start preparing your toppings.

    • Toppings Preparation

  • Wrinse your pepper and boil it in water. It's being boiled first before blending because its going to be added directly on your pizza.
  • Chop your bell pepper, onions, and spring onions
  • Boil you meat with no water, add onions, fannesse seed, seasoning and cover it to boil.
  • Dice and fry the hotdogs with no seasoning, then blend your boiled and unseasoned boiled pepper.
  • Dice your onions really tiny so as to caramelize it
  • Cook the blended pepper and add all the necessary seasonings to the peppersoup, suya spice, sweet chilli sauce, maggi and salt.
  • Make sure your mozzarella cheese is in a fridge or freezer. Chop it into strips and set aside.
  • Cut your dough into small pieces and use your rolling pin to make it flat, but not too thin.
  • Add your pizza dough to the oven and add your toppings with the shredded cheese, then put in the oven.
  • Leave for some minutes in the and then remove from the oven
  • Enjoy your fresh pizza!!!

Vanilla & Chocolate cake

Ingredients(cake)

  • 500g of Flour
  • Baking Powder 2tbsp
  • Butter/ Magarine 400g
  • Sugar 250g
  • Egg 7
  • Milk
  • Butter scotch

Preparation (Cake)

  • Measure your flour into a bowl
  • Measure your sugar and butter in a bowl and whisk till its creamy and fluffy
  • Break your eggs into a separate bowl 7 eggs
  • Whisk your eggs and milk sure it foams very well
  • Pair in your eggs to the butter
  • Add butter scothch to the butter batter and vanilla extract (1 cover)
  • Measure your milk(120ml * 3) = 1 cup
  • Add your milk to mixture.
  • Wash and dry your cake pan, grease the pan lightly with magarine especially to the edges of the pan to avoid stickiness.
  • Sprinkle a bit of flour to the cake pan to make the cake come out intact. Set the pan aside.
  • Then add your dry ingredient (Flour) into the butter mix. Whisk together and pour to the cake pan.

    • Gas Method:

  • Get a large pot and pour dry salt inside, spread it evenly
  • If you have a thermometer preheat it till its 120oC
  • Add your cake pan into the heated pot (Oven) and cover for about 45 minutes.
  • Dont check your cake too often and leave to bake
  • After 35 minutes use a skewer to check if it has baked all through
  • Set your cake aside for icing!!!

Ingredients(Chocolate garnash/ syrup)

  • 60g of chocolate powder (Unsweetened)
  • 1cup of heavy cream (Hollandia milk)
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp of magarine
  • 2 tbsp of con flour

Preparation (Chocolate garnash)

  • Get a Clean pot and pour your cocoa powder, add your corn flour, then your sugar and mix together
  • Gradually add your heavy cream (Hollandia) and mix to avoid lumps.
  • Place your pot on stove with low heat and stirr
  • Check your garnash if it has thickened as you keep stirring. The add your vanilla extract
  • Set aside for later!!

Ingredients(Butter Cream)

  • 300g of Butter
  • 1kg Icing Sugar
  • 5 tbsp water
  • Bread knife
  • Scrapper
  • Cake tray

Preparation (Butter Cream)

  • Measure your butter and add into a bowl, whisk till its fluffy with a turning stick.
  • Then gradually add your icing sugar bit by bit and stirr continually
  • Add 5 tbsp of water after the butter and icing sugar hardened to ease the mixture
  • Add your flavors: vanilla, butter scotch and mix it together.
  • Leave for about 10 - 15 minutes
  • Clean your cake tray very well and spread abit of the butter cream in the middle of the tray where the cake will rest. This is to make the cake stick t the tray without falling off.
  • Add the first batch of your cake unto the tray
  • Get your serrated knife/ breadknife and use it to level the cake and make it straight
  • Get your piping bag and cut off the buttom, then begin to fill it with some of the butter cream. NB: Not all
  • Add your butter cream onto the first batch of the cake and create a bridge by adding the butter cream on top of the cake, right at the tip.
  • Then add your chocolate garnash to the cake in the middle of the bridge.
  • Stack your cakes and repeat this procedure, use the trimmed cake to make it balanced if it doesnt look straight
  • Gently shape the side of your cake with the bread and make it even all round.
  • Separate some butter cream into another bowl and spread round your cake
  • Be generous with your butter cream
  • Get another bowl with warm water and dip your scrapper inside
  • With the smooth part, begin to scrape the excess part with it
  • MAke sure all sides are even, then use the other side of the scrapper to add some style to the cake
  • pour your remaining chocolate garnash or syrup into a dripper and pour to the sides of the cake to create a pouring effect.
  • Add sprinkles to the top of your Cake
  • Enjoy your cake!!!
